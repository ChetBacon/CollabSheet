collabsheet

an analog collaboration

Hey there! Good chance you landed on this site because you're a big fan of shooting analogue film. We are too, so great that you've found us!

The more often you hear that clicking sound of your camera's shutter, the more you'll be able to receive feedback on your photo's, and thereby increasing your skill as a photographer. And getting the best out of your camera and film is what we all want right?

To keep advancing isn't always as easy though; A lack of inspiration, a shortage of events to capture, or simply a couch that is too comfortable can hinder you with that.. What we're going to offer you here is a fun way to get you into the field again with a fresh mind full of new ideas.

In a nutshell, CollabSheet is a photography platform/website in which you can subscribe to a specific project, are provided with a piece of 35mm film, and a ton of inspiration. We're offering you the raw material and a surplus of food for thought, but the magical creative part is all on your side. The goal of a project is easy: shoot each of the 36 shots with your interpretation of the project's theme, and hand your film over to us. Check the site later to see your own photo's, and that of all the other users'.

This way, CollabSheet both gives you a ton of new ideas to shoot with, and an active community that inspires, gives you feedback, and let's you share ideas with. So hit that subscribe button and let's unite and experience analogue photography together!
