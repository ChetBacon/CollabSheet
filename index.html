introduction

Getting the best out of your camera and film is what we all want right? The more often you hear that clicking sound of your camera's shutter, the more you'll be able to enjoy your hobby.

Shooting your roll of film isn't always as easy though; A lack of inspiration, a shortage of events to capture, or simply a couch that is too comfortable can hinder you with that..

collabsheet offers you a fun way to get you into the field again with a fresh mind full of new ideas!

How does it work?

For the past weeks, we have develop a concept

With our team we think about an interesting project that will inspire you to shoot great photos.

You have a couple of week to shoot the project, go get 'm tiger! '

Develop your film at your local filmlab before the project's due date

Upload all your scanned photos to the collabsheet website

Visit the website and enjoy the uploaded photos from all users on one collabsheet
